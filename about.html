About Abcorn Tobacco

Who are we?

  • Abcorn Tobacco is Class ' A ' certified merchants licensed with the regulator for tobacco in Zimbabwe ( Tobacco Industry & Marketing Board - TIMB) to purchase raw virginia tobacco from the auction floors for International exports...
  • We are exporters of raw Virginia Flue Cured Tobacco
  • We are licenced to trade in the biggest auction floor in the world BOKA tobacco auction floors, TSF, and Premier tobacco auction floors
  • We are also licenced contractors where we contract farmers under prior arrangements on farming inputs, funding, and requirements for their crop
  • We are processors of raw leaf tobacco and further enhance it to Cutrag tobacco for export
  • Our core business is to deliver quality tobacco to our clients around the world.

What we do

  • We enter into arrangements with our clients around the world to buy tobacco from the Auction Floors on their behalf...
  • We also source tobacco for our our clients by way of engaging into contract farming to deliver quality grade tobacco at a nominal cost
  • We stock tobacco for resell
  • We process tobacco & manufacturers of Cut rag tobacco for export
  • Do you want to purchase tobacco? As an international client there are three ways we can assist you for the purchase of tobacco cost effectively:
    1. Short term
      • You can purchase directly from a range of what we have in stock. This model is not as cost effective as the other two models the medium and long term but is very fast
    2. Medium term
      • We can enter into arrangements for you to purchase from the best auction floors in the world using our licenses and influence.
      • This model is cost effective and gives you the pleasure of choosing your exact grades from various farmers selling their crop.
    3. Long term - Contract Farming
      • This is the most cost effective model.
      • As an international client we can enter into contract farming arrangements with various farmers to farm on our behalf where we provide them with the funding and inputs, in which they will be bound to sell only to you at an agreed price.
      • We have our team that will be monitoring progress and provide further technical assistance to the farmers in all regions
